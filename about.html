Company Profile

Sohul Al Diraiya commenced operation as a service provider and supplier of Electromechanical and power electronics equipment of various manufacturer brands.


As of Today, we are providing services to our valueable clients as listed below:

  • Electromechanical work for Data Rooms & Power Rooms.
  • Supply, Installation & commissioning of Generators.
  • Supply, Installation & commissioning of UPS including battery backup, Changeover switches & distribution panels.
  • Supply, Installation & commissioning of Rectifiers.
  • Supply, Installation & commissioning of Fire Detection & Suppression System(FM200, NOVIC,CO2)
  • Supply, Installation & commissioning of HVAC System.
  • Supply, Installation & commissioning of Generators.
  • Supply & Installation of CCTV & Building Access Control System.
  • Supply & Installation of Raised Floor.
  • Industrial Electrification.
  • Building Electrification & Solar Energy System.
